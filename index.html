I am currently reading Stephen King's On Writing: A Memoir of the Craft in support of the second pillar, How To Write. I did not know that he struggled with drugs and alcohol. I have actually never read a Stephen King book as, frankly, I am too much of a chicken to partake in the horror genre of anything. But, I may have to attempt it. It is interesting to read about how he crafts his characters and stories, and I think I may need to see the real deal to understand his methods. First and foremost, Stephen King considers himself a storyteller, and there is a lot in On Writing that can lend itself to being able to tell stories in other mediums.
